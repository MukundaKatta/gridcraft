# Gridcraft

> An Instagram grid that actually looks good.

Plan six months of posts visually. See your grid before you post it. Never break the aesthetic again.

## What you get

- **Drag and drop** — Rearrange posts until the grid flows. Save layouts. Undo freely.
- **Auto color-matched** — Suggests edits to keep your palette consistent across posts.
- **Schedules natively** — Once the grid is locked, we queue everything into your Meta Business Suite.
